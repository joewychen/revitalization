Cord/Wires Organizer


Step 1

Collect empty toilet paper rolls and a shoe boxe: Use a number of empty rolls that fits into the shoe box. For most boxes, 16-20 rolls should be enough. I used 20 rolls in total (2 for the bigger cords, and 18 for my smaller cords). Other materials: Glue or tape that works well with paper, scissors, piece of cardbaord in similar size to the box's interior. Once you've collected the material, you're ready to start!


Step 2

Create a divider using another piece of cardboard: First, measure the length and height of the box. Then, cut the cardboard into the measured dimensions. Finally, glue the sides of the cardboard to the walls of the box as in the picture.


Step 3

Stack the rolls into the larger side of the box until it is filled. These tubes will be used to store the smaller cords. You're finished! Now you can start putting in the cords.
